E-Commerce Customer & Sales Analysis

Tools: SQL Server, Power BI, Power Query, Notion

Dataset: 250,000 transactions · 49,661 customers · Jan 2020 – Sep 2023

An end-to-end business analysis project investigating customer churn, revenue concentration, and retention opportunities for an e-commerce business across 4 product categories.
Key findings: Loyal and Champion customers drive 64.29% of revenue despite being 46.70% of the base. Overall churn is 19.99%, with the 18-24 segment being the most at-risk. A 40.59% return rate is structural, flat across every dimension, pointing to fulfillment or product quality rather than customer behavior.
Analysis covers revenue KPIs, age and gender segmentation, RFM modeling using CTEs and NTILE scoring, churn predictor analysis, and a 4-page Power BI dashboard with DAX measures, Pareto charts, and heatmaps. Findings are compiled into a full executive report with 7 prioritized recommendations.
